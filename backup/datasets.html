Temporal Popularity Image Collection (TPIC)

https://github.com/social-media-prediction/TPIC2017

The SMP datasets TPIC2017 contains 680K photos, which collected from Flickr (a photo sharing platform) and corresponding anonymized photo-sharing records ranges of 3 years.
